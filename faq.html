Frequently Asked Questions

Do you bulk-bill kids?

Yes! All children under 16 with Medicare are bulk-billed for check-ups and most treatments.

What if I’m really scared of the dentist?

We specialise in nervous patients — happy gas, music, blankets, and zero judgement.

Can I get an appointment today?

Yes — emergency same-day appointments always available.
